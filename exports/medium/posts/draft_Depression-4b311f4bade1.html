Depression

I love reddit.com/r/2meirl4meirl in a shameful way. 

It’s a subreddit filled with self-deprecating, suicidal, depressing pictures and things that are laughable at first glance until you feel a twisting in your gut that says: “wait a second why do i completely empathize with this”

In my limited perspective, I’ve noticed that self-deprecating humor is on the rise. I realize that this is probably due to my tastes, and that I’ve put myself in an echo chamber of humor that pokes fun at the fact that I kind of hate myself. I don’t think I’m alone in this, as I know many peers also love these kinds of too-real feelings.

These are a couple of images from the most popular posts from /r/2meirl4meirl, but I’ve seen many similar posts on other subreddits.

I think it’s something to note that people my age are so obsessed with making it clear that they do not think they’re perfect. I’m going to continue to guess at the fact why this is despite not being a psychologist, sociologist, or have any sort of education about the human mind and how it works.
